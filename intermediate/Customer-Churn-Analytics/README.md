## Objective

The goal of this dashboard suite is to provide a complete view of customer churn patterns and enable data-driven retention strategies by analyzing:

| | |
|:---:|:---:|
| **Customer Base** | Total customers · Active vs Churned |
| **Revenue Impact** | Total revenue · Revenue lost to churn |
| **Risk Factors** | Contract type · Internet service · Payment method · Support services |
| **Customer Segments** | High risk · Loyal · High revenue |
| **Root Causes** | Contract · Internet service · Online security |
| **Retention Actions** | Targeted strategies for at-risk segments |

## Key Insights

| Insight | Finding |
|---------|---------|
| **Churn Rate** | 26.5% overall churn rate with $2.86M revenue lost |
| **Highest Risk Segment** | Month-to-month contracts drive 42.7% churn risk (vs 2.8% for Two-year) |
| **Riskiest Combination** | Month-to-month + Fiber optic = 54.6% churn rate |
| **Key Influencers** | Month-to-month contract, No online security, No tech support, Electronic check payment |
| **Fiber Optic Risk** | 41.9% churn rate despite being highest revenue contributor |
| **Average Churn Profile** | Customers churn at ~18 months tenure with $74.44 monthly charges |
| **Tech Support Gap** | 49.3% of customers lack tech support - a key churn driver |
| **Payment Risk** | Electronic check users show highest churn among payment methods |

---

## Recommended Retention Actions

| Priority | Action | Target Segment |
|----------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | Convert Month-to-month users to annual plans | Month-to-month contract holders |
| 2 | Promote Tech Support bundles | Customers without tech support |
| 3 | Prioritize Fiber Optic retention | Fiber optic internet users |
| 4 | Reward loyal customers | Long-tenure active customers |
| 5 | Target high-value accounts before churn | High revenue customers approaching ~18 months |
